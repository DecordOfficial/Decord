# Voice-Control-Plus

## What does it do?

It lets you automatically disconnect the person you choose from the voice channel at all times. It can also keep them muted or deafened in the voice channel automatically.

## How to use

After you pick the target user, right-click them and click the button for the action you want (disconnect, mute, or deafen) in the menu, and perform the disconnect/mute/deafen once yourself. After that, the plugin keeps applying that action to that user.

## How to turn it off

Open that user’s menu again and click the button that shows as active; the feature turns off.

Have fun :)
